Entry Schemes
After 12th
NDA – National Defence Academy
Most prestigious entry. UPSC exam twice a year. Training at NDA Pune then IMA Dehradun.
Age: 16.5–19.5PCM requiredUPSC exam
After 12th
TES – Technical Entry Scheme
For PCM students with ≥70%. 5-year B.Tech at CME/MCTE. No written exam.
Age: 16.5–19.5PCM ≥70%
Graduate
IMA via CDS (Permanent Commission)
UPSC CDS exam. Permanent Commission as officer. Biannual notification.
Age: 19–24Any degree
Graduate
OTA via CDS / UPSC (SSC)
Short Service Commission at OTA Chennai. Open to men and women. 10–14 year commission.
Age: 19–25Graduate
Professional
JAG – Judge Advocate General
Legal branch of the Indian Army. Men and women eligible. PC and SSC available.
Age: 21–27LLB/LLM
JCO / OR
Soldier / JCO Entries
Rally-based recruitment: GD, Clerk, Technical, Tradesman, Nursing Assistant.
Age: 17.5–238th–12th
After 12th
NDA – Naval Wing
Entry into Naval Wing at NDA via UPSC. Executive or engineering branches.
Age: 16.5–19.5PCM required
After 12th
10+2 B.Tech Entry (Cadet)
4-year B.Tech at Naval Academy Ezhimala. Direct commission on completion.
Age: 17–19PCM ≥70%
Graduate
CDS – Executive / Technical Branch
UPSC CDS for executive branch. Technical graduates apply directly.
Age: 19–24BE/B.Tech
Graduate
INET – Indian Naval Entrance Test
Navy's biannual own entrance exam. SSB interview follows selection.
Age: 19–25Graduate
SSC
Short Service Commission
10–14 year commission for law, education, logistics, and IT branches.
Age: 19–27Degree required
Sailor
MR / AA / SSR Entries
Matric Recruit, Artificer Apprentice, Senior Secondary Recruit for below-officer roles.
Age: 17–2010th / 12th
After 12th
NDA – Air Force Wing
Entry into Air Force Wing at NDA via UPSC. Flying or ground duty branches.
Age: 16.5–19.5PCM required
Graduate
AFCAT – Air Force Common Admission Test
Biannual IAF exam for Flying and Ground Duty (Technical/Non-Technical) branches.
Age: 20–24Degree / B.Tech
Graduate
CDS – Air Force Academy (Flying Branch)
UPSC CDS for Flying Branch. AFA Dundigal. Permanent Commission.
Age: 19–23PCM graduate
Specialist
Meteorology Branch
For MSc Physics/Maths/CS graduates. Short Service Commission.
Age: 20–26MSc Science
SSC
SSC Women Entry (Flying)
SSC for women in Flying (Helicopter, Transport) and Ground Duty via AFCAT.
Age: 20–24Degree required
Airmen
Airmen Group X & Y
Group X (Technical) and Group Y (Non-Technical). Largest below-officer entry.
